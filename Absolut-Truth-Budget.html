Reality-check notes

  1. Vir Das revised ₹7.98cr to ₹3cr. International booking ~$150k–$299k/appearance; an Indian OTT host package is negotiated, not 6× a US one-off.
  2. Casting revised ₹84.2L to ₹3L (original appears to be a data-entry error; casting fees run a few lakh).
  3. Added Research and Graphics/footage-licensing per episode, since an investigative-comedy show is research- and graphics-heavy. ≈₹33L/ep sits inside the ₹50L–1cr reality-show band (Business Standard).
  4. Guest fees itemised above, single-appearance estimates, mixed tier. Nikhil Kamath modelled at ₹0.
  5. Media benchmarks: YouTube masthead ₹1–10L/day (The Media Ant); premium Mumbai OOH ₹3–12L/site/month (BuyMediaSpace).
  6. Where the original gave a range, the lower figure is used. Figures are indicative for portfolio illustration, not quotes.
